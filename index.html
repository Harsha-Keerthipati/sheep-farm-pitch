Zero Land Dependency — This Is Not Traditional Farming

Why TMR + slatted floor

No Grazing Land Needed

100% dry Total Mixed Ration feed. No green fodder cultivation. No monsoon dependency. No crop failure risk. Feed is sourced commercially from Tirupati / Nellore mills.

Elevated Slatted Floor

Animals live on plastic slatted platforms 4 feet above ground. Urine drains away instantly. Manure stays dry. Ammonia buildup eliminated. Disease risk reduced to baseline minimum vs ground-level farming.

All-In-All-Out System

Every batch enters and exits together. Between batches: full pressure wash, Formalin disinfection, 7-day dry fallow. No disease carryover. Commercial feedlot logic applied to small ruminants.

Why Nellore Breed

  • Purebred Nellore (Jodipi / Brown strain) ram lambs are the premium meat breed of Andhra Pradesh.
  • Festival buyers — especially Sankranti consumers — specifically seek Nellore genetics.
  • Sourced from Rajampet and Proddatur shandies, the main Nellore lamb markets in the region.
  • At 31 kg finish, well-conformed TMR-fed Nellore rams command a ₹30–50/kg premium over pasture-fed crossbreds.

Risk & Mitigation

The Risks — And Why They Are Manageable

Investors trust plans that acknowledge risk more than plans that pretend there is none. Each risk below is paired with its likelihood, impact, and the specific mitigation in place.

Low Medium High

Lamb procurement price higher than ₹520/kg

Likelihood: Medium Impact: High

Field price verification at Rajampet / Proddatur shandy before finalising. Buy price and sell price move together — margin compresses but doesn't collapse.

Festival sell price disappoints (Scenario C)

Likelihood: Low–Medium Impact: Medium

₹95,580 maximum modelled loss. Covered by owner's personal reserve. Does not threaten infrastructure or Batch 2.

Feed price spike (maize / cottonseed)

Likelihood: Medium Impact: Medium

₹35,000 contingency buffer in OpEx. At 250-lamb scale, bulk wholesale rate drops from ₹24 to ₹20/kg, building in structural protection.

Animal health outbreak

Likelihood: Low Impact: High

All-In-All-Out system, full vaccination protocol (PPR, ET + booster, dual deworming), live-in caretaker, 10-day biosecurity reset between batches.

Borewell failure

Likelihood: Very Low Impact: Medium

Strong local water table confirmed. Hill catchment surroundings. Neighbour bore data validates depth and yield.

No direct buyers — forced trader sale

Likelihood: Medium (Batch 1) Impact: Medium

Trader sale still generates revenue — just at lower margin. Direct buyer outreach begins 3 months before Batch 1 exit. Tirupati shops are the primary target.

Four channels being built in parallel — each de-risks dependence on traders.

Batch 2

Bangalore Telugu Meat Traders

KR Market. AP migrant population specifically seeks genuine Nellore genetics unavailable locally in Karnataka. Premium of ₹20–40/kg above AP rates. Transport ~₹10,000 for 50 animals — math works comfortably.

Batch 3

Hotel & Restaurant Supply

Mid-range hotels near the Tirupati temple precinct. TMR-fed animals have more consistent fat cover and conformation — better portioning for kitchens. A trial of 10–15 animals opens the relationship.

All Batches

WhatsApp Business + Facebook farm updates from October. AP / Telangana consumers increasingly prefer buying direct from visible farms for Sankranti. Farm pickup or Tirupati delivery. No middleman margin.
